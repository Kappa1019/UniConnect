What Problem Are We Solving?

Students at premier Indian institutes (IITs, NITs, IIITs, and top universities) struggle to find timely and consolidated information about internships, job opportunities, hackathons, cultural fests, and coding competitions. Information is scattered across multiple sources, so students often miss out on valuable experiences that could enhance their skills, careers, and campus life.

  • Timely updates about opportunities are hard to find.
  • Information is scattered across websites, groups, and social feeds.
  • Students miss out on internships, hackathons, jobs, and events that could help them grow.
  • This lack of centralization makes it hard for students to plan and participate fully in campus life.
